About Mel

I’m a software developer by trade, but I work to share stories more than anything else. I am easy-going, adaptable, and collaborative; when I see a need, I try to help fulfill it. I'm from Chicago, but I'm excited to move to Orlando with my girlfriend and our two cats in 2019. I'd love to get a beer and talk about RuPaul's Drag Race or women's soccer, anytime.

I believe in the power of human connection, and that it is incredibly important to create connections with people, organizations, causes, and cultures around the world. Technology and the internet both have incredible power over shaping our future, and I’d like to work to ensure that our world continues to become a more supportive, better educated, and more thoughtful place.
